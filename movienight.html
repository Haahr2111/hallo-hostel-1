Sunday movie night in the café

19:00 - Sunday - October 14th 2018

The reception at Hallo hostel

Hallo dear guests! We, Hallo Hostel, would like to invite you to some traditional Danish "hygge".
We are transforming our "Hallo café", to a "Hallo movie theater".
On the big screen we are showing the pop culture flick "pulp fiction" starring John Travolta, and Samuel L Jackson.
Bring your friends, or come alone and make some new friends!
Snacks and drinks can be purchased at the bar.
See you there!
